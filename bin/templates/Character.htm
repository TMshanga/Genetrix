Appearance:

What does the character look like? What is the history or symbolism behind their appearance? Are the colours significant? Are the clothing materials and accessories significant?
You may also explore their gestures, movements and speech with how they too reflect their character.

Personality & Character Development:

What are the characters positive and negative characteristics? What (both) positive and negative characteristics does the character gain as the story develops? How does the character want to be perceived, as opposed to how they are truly perceived? What unique, unexpected quirks (of the character specifically) reveal themselves as the story progresses?
Consider G. Jung's (and Briggs Myers) proposed personality categories: Extraverted vs. Introverted, Sensing vs. Intuition, Thinking vs. Feeling or Judging vs. Perceiving. Unique combinations of the 4 dichotomies will produce up to 16 distinct personalities.
You may also be worth exploring the 5-man-band trope, or the Alignment System.  A unique character may be created from purposefully subverting tropes.
How do their actions and dialogue within the story thus reflect their personality, as well as their character development? Why does their character develop?

External Conflicts:

What are the conflicts the character faces with the outside world, like nature or a character? Why are these conflicts significant to the character? How do they generate tension and suspense and impact the plot?
What do they want as opposed to what they need?

Internal Conflicts:

What psychological conflicts does the character face with themselves? Why are these conflicts significant to the character? How do they generate tension and suspense and impact the plot?
What do they want as opposed to what they need?

Background:

Where does you character come from? How does this influence their personality and their external/internal conflicts? What motifs and allusions may lie in their backstory and how are these significant? What character development may have been undergone already and why?

Skills & Shortcomings:

What are their abilities and corresponding weaknesses? How are they related to each other? Why are they present?

Other:

What other information might you need? This may be trivia.
For instance, what is out first impression of the character in the story and how will the impression be achieved?
